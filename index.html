About Us

Sweet Life Ice-cream shop is a passion project that was founded with the belief that life is always better with ice-cream. We use only the finest and freshest ingredients to create our classic and unique flavors, and we offer a wide range of toppings and mix-ins to create the perfect custom treat. Our goal is to provide our customers with a memorable experience, whether they're stopping by for a mid-day snack or an after-dinner dessert. We take pride in the quality of our products, the cleanliness of our store, and the friendliness of our staff, and we aim to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all our guests to enjoy.

Our Products

Chocolate ice cream with cherry is a delicious frozen dessert that combines the rich, creamy flavor of chocolate with the sweet and tangy taste of cherries. It is typically made by churning a mixture of milk, cream, sugar, and cocoa powder in an ice cream maker until it reaches a smooth and creamy consistency, and then folding in chopped or pureed cherries. Some variations may also include chocolate chips or fudge swirls for added texture and flavor. This dessert is a classic favorite that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with other desserts like cakes, pies, or waffles.

Blueberry-lavender ice cream is a delicious and unique frozen dessert that combines the tartness of blueberries with the floral aroma and flavor of lavender. This ice cream is typically made with a creamy vanilla or custard base that is infused with fresh blueberries and a touch of lavender extract or dried lavender flowers. The result is a smooth and creamy ice cream with a slightly fruity and floral taste that is perfect for enjoying on a hot summer day or as a refreshing dessert after a meal. Blueberry-lavender ice cream is a popular choice for those who enjoy experimenting with new flavor combinations and want to try something different from the usual vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry options.

Crazy mint chocolate shake ice cream is a delicious frozen dessert that combines the flavors of mint and chocolate in a creamy milkshake-like consistency. The ice cream is typically made with a base of vanilla ice cream, which is blended with fresh mint leaves, chocolate chips or chunks, and sometimes other flavorings like peppermint extract or fudge sauce. The result is a refreshing and indulgent treat that is perfect for hot summer days or any time you're in the mood for a sweet, minty treat.

No churn cotton candy ice cream is a sweet and creamy frozen dessert that is easy to make at home without an ice cream maker. This ice cream gets its unique flavor from cotton candy, which is melted and mixed into the ice cream base. The ice cream base typically includes sweetened condensed milk and heavy cream, which are whipped together until thick and creamy. The cotton candy is melted down and added to the mixture, giving the ice cream its distinct pink color and sweet, candy-like flavor.

Vegan Caramel Ice-cream is a creamy and delicious frozen dessert that is made without any animal products. It is typically made with a base of non-dairy milk, such as almond milk or coconut milk, that is then sweetened with sugar or a natural sweetener like maple syrup or agave nectar. To achieve the caramel flavor, a mixture of sugar and water is cooked until it caramelizes, then added to the ice cream base. The mixture is then blended until smooth and churned in an ice cream maker to create a creamy and smooth texture.
